# Restov

A major city in southern [Brevoy](brevoy.md), located in the Rostland region.

## The Swordlords of Restov

The Swordlords are the nobility and ruling class of Rostland. They hold significant political power within [Brevoy](brevoy.md), particularly in the southern Rostland region.

### The Greenbelt Charter

The Swordlords have issued a charter to explore and travel the Greenbelt, a forested hilly area south of Rostland.

## Geography

Restov is located in:
- Southern [Brevoy](brevoy.md)
- The Rostland region
- North of the Greenbelt
