# Brevoy

A nation split between two distinct regions with ongoing political tensions.

## Geography

### Northern Region: Issia
The northern portion of Brevoy.

### Southern Region: Rostland
The southern portion of Brevoy. Home to [Restov](restov.md), a major city and seat of power for the Swordlords.

## Political Structure

The nation experiences ongoing tensions between its two regions (Issia and Rostland), with the [Swordlords of Restov](restov.md) being a particularly powerful political faction in Rostland.
